# Unimad

## Overview

Unimad helps Pakistani students find the right university without visiting 20 different websites.

## Problem

- University websites in Pakistan are outdated, hard to navigate, and inconsistent. Students and their families waste weeks collecting basic information that should take minutes.
- Most end up relying on word of mouth or whatever their coaching center recommends, which means they miss options that might be a better fit.
- This is especially painful during admission season when deadlines overlap and missing one by a day means waiting another year

## Goals

1. A public directory starting with the top 10-20 most sought-after universities in Pakistan.
2. A details page with more details (program overview, key deadline, fee structure).

## Core User Flow

1. A student visits Unimad.
2. Searches by program, degree type (bachelors, masters, PhD), or study mode (online, on-campus)
3. They get a clean directory of universities showing the information that actually matters: deadlines, fees, eligibility, and a direct link to the official university page for more details.
4. That's it. No accounts needed. No login walls. Just search and find.
